Describing Myself:

I don't like photos, but I do love learning, and I'm going back to my "tech" roots. I've spent a number of years in the business world, doing various techie and non-techie things, went back to school for my M.S. in Education, and became a certified teacher. I spent 18 years teaching, and now I'm taking a great boot-camp through the Tech Workforce Program, TechTown, and SavvyCoders, and it's going to be a good ride!


I've wanted to get back deeper into tech for a long time. I've always used computers, have taught technology classes at the high school level, used tech for creating my lesson plans, Powerpoints for teaching. There's never enough time as a teacher, to explore other things.
