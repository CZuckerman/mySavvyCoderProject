Describing Myself:

I love learning, and I'm going back to my "tech" roots. I've spent a number of years in the business world, doing various techie and non-techie things, went back to school for my M.S. in Education, and became a certified teacher. I spent 18 years teaching, and now I'm taking a great boot-camp through the Tech Workforce Program, TechTown, and SavvyCoders, and it's a good ride!

I've wanted to get back deeper into tech for a long time. I've always used computers, have taught technology classes at the high school level, used tech for creating my lesson plans, developed PowerPoint presentations for teaching. There's never enough time, as a teacher, to explore other things.


A few quick facts about me:

  • I'm fluent in English, American Sign Language and somewhat receptively fluent in Spanish
  • I'm Deaf, so if I don't answer you, the most likely answer is that I didn't hear you
  • I love learning, and sometimes I fall down a rabbit-hole so I use a timer to keep myself on track
